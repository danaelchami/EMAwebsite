FAQ

Questions & answers

Is EMA free to use?

Yes, EMA offers a free tier with essential features. We also offer premium plans for users who need advanced capabilities and higher usage limits.

Is my email data secure?

Absolutely. EMA uses industry-standard encryption and never stores the content of your emails on our servers. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Which email providers are supported?

EMA currently works with Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo Mail. We're actively working on adding support for additional email providers.

Do I need to create an account?

A simple account is required to use EMA, which helps us provide a personalized experience and save your preferences across devices.

How accurate are the AI summaries?

Our AI models are trained on millions of emails and continuously improved. Summaries are highly accurate for most business and personal emails.

Does EMA work offline?

While some basic features may work without an internet connection, EMA requires connectivity to access its AI capabilities and synchronize with your email providers.
